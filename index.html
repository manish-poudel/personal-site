My Journey

The Beginning

I have been developing apps for over five years across multiple domains, creating solutions for users while working for companies — and often for my own sake. It started with learning Java to develop a simple Android game called ThumbBomb with one of my brothers. That fun and creative experience propelled me into this journey.

Breakthrough

The pivotal moment was developing a telemedicine app called Talk My Doc, even before COVID-19. We freshly learned Flutter to build for both iOS and Android, which helped us move fast and adapt to the changing needs of patients and doctors.

Startup Experience

To support the app, we registered a company and I personally handled contracts with banks, tax procedures, and third-party integrations like eSewa. Although the app didn’t succeed commercially, it taught me valuable lessons about solving real-world problems and building scalable systems.

Professional Work

At Truenary, I contributed to several projects using both Android Native and Flutter. My responsibilities included feature development, debugging, and automating deployment workflows. This role helped me solidify my understanding of software architecture and real-world project dynamics.

Personal & Freelancing Projects

  • Travelmotto: A travel gaming app designed to make exploring new places fun and interactive.
  • Imagly: An AI-powered OCR dictionary that helps readers get word meanings by scanning text with their phone.
  • Investment Decisions using LLM & Data Science: I applied Large Language Models and data science techniques to make informed investment decisions.

Lifelong Learning
